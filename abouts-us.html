About MediQlogix

MediQlogix is a leading provider of innovative, cloud-based solutions for healthcare quality management, compliance, and clinical analytics. Our platform empowers organizations to improve patient outcomes, drive operational excellence, and support continuous growth through advanced technology and expert support.

Learn More

Company Profile

Founded in 2004 by Deepak Adoni, MediQlogix puts patients first, embedding safety, evidence-based care, and ongoing performance monitoring into every aspect of our platform. Our advanced AI-driven quality governance automates compliance and risk detection, while clinical-grade analytics deliver timely, validated insights from unified healthcare data—enabling confident, rapid decision-making and a continuous cycle of quality improvement.

The MediQM platform leverages cloud technology and AI to help your teams optimize performance, maintain compliance, and monitor patient outcomes in real time—all with secure mobile access and seamless collaboration. Our suite includes integrated Revenue Lifecycle Management (RCS) for financial efficiency, advanced analytics for proactive risk detection, and Learn MediQ—an AI-powered training tool that streamlines onboarding and boosts productivity, ensuring your organization excels in quality, safety, and overall performance.

Vision and Mission

We are committed to empowering hospital and healthcare organisations with innovative AI and ML solutions to enhance patient care and increase operational efficiency through advanced technology and the expertise of professionals. With our advanced software, we develop data-driven insights for medical professionals, reducing the administrative load in delivering care to patients in the long run.

Our Vision

Empower healthcare organisations with innovative AI solutions for better operations. Enhance decision-making through data-driven insights and intelligent automation. Streamline workflows to reduce administrative burden and improve patient outcomes. Build a future where technology and compassion go hand in hand in delivering care.

Our Mission

Transform healthcare with advanced technological solutions for better patient care and operational efficiency. Empower medical professionals with real-time insights and intelligent automation. Drive innovation that ensures accessible, reliable, and future-ready healthcare systems. Foster a connected ecosystem where technology bridges gaps in care, communication, and outcomes.
